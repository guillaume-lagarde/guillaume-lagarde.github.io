Generative Art

If you would like to buy a piece, the easiest way is to send me a message via instagram or linkedin. Same if you would like to work on a project with me, do not hesitate to send me a message.

In a nutshell:

What: During my free time I like to draw, on a highly irregular schedule, using programming languages like processing but also pen plotters.

Where: I have a small instagram account dedicated to this.

Why: The primary reason for me to have started these creations was to balance the unbiased flavor of science with a bit of bias inherent to art. Since then I have discovered two things: first, I am not alone there is an amazing and vibrant community and second, it is slowly but surely becoming part of my beeing to do this.
