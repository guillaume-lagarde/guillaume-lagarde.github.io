Practical Session 2: Reinforcement Learning with MCTS in Tic-Tac-Toe and Connect Four

In this practical session, we will delve into the realm of reinforcement learning and apply it to the classic games of Tic-Tac-Toe and Connect Four. We will implement Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS), a powerful algorithm that enables agents to make efficient decisions in complex environments. This algorithm is also one of the main core of AlphaGo.
