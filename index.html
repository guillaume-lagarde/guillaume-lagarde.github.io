About me

I am an associate professor in computer science at Bordeaux University and LaBRI, member of the teams Logical foundations of computing and Synthesis.

My research interests lie mainly at the intersection between algorithms and complexity theory.

Short Bio

  • 2023 - present: Associate Professor in computer science at Bordeaux University and LaBRI, member of the team Logical foundations of computing and Synthesis.
  • 2021 - 2023: Research Engineer at Criteo where I was designing large-scale recommendation systems and putting them in production.
  • 2019 - 2021: Postdoc at LaBRI, Bordeaux, in the DeepSynth CNRS Momentum project, with Nathanaël Fijalkow.
  • 2018 - 2019: Postdoc in the Approximation and Proof Complexity group at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, hosted by Jakob Nordström, Johan Håstad, and Per Austrin.
  • 2015 - 2018: PhD candidate at IRIF, University Paris Diderot, supervised by Sophie Laplante and Sylvain Perifel. Thesis Manuscript.
