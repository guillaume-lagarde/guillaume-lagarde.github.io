Habits Tracker

Welcome to Habit Tracker!

Habit Tracker is a minimalistic web page designed to assist you in monitoring and improving your daily habits. Unlike other applications, Habit Tracker doesn't rely on predefined templates; instead, it embraces a flexible, user-centric approach. You create and maintain your logs in any text editor of your choice. Once you've completed your entries, simply copy and paste them into the designated area within this page. This approach guarantees a user experience uniquely tailored to your personal needs.

Getting Started: Define Your Keys

Start your tracking journey by defining your keys. Each key represents a habit or an activity you wish to monitor. You can designate any symbol, letter, or word as a key. The key is the heart of your tracking process, so make sure it's intuitive!

Types of Keys

In Habit Tracker, the keys used to log habits fall into two categories:

  • Boolean: Keys followed by a comma fall under this category. When a Boolean key shows up in a log, it signifies that the corresponding habit was undertaken that day (e.g., "R" for restaurant). Boolean keys are tracked by their occurrences. You can also annotate these keys with comments by placing the comment after the key, separated by a comma.
  • By Value: These keys are defined with a colon. They express the count or amount associated with the habit for a particular day.
